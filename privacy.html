Privacy Policy for Topsy Stack

Effective Date: June 29, 2026

Topsy Stack is a casual mobile game. This policy explains what information may be processed when you use the app.

Information We Process

The game may use device identifiers, including advertising identifiers when permission is granted, to support third-party advertising and ad measurement. Advertising partners may also process ad interaction data, impression data, and technical information needed to deliver and measure ads.

The game stores local gameplay preferences and progress on your device, such as sound settings, music settings, completed levels, and similar gameplay state. This local data is used to make the game work properly and is not intended to identify you.

App Tracking Transparency

On supported iOS versions, the app may ask for permission to track activity across apps and websites owned by other companies. You can deny this permission and still play the game. If you later want to change the permission, use the iOS Settings app.

Third-Party Services

The app may include advertising or analytics-related SDKs, such as Chartboost, Yandex Mobile Ads, Vungle, InMobi, and AppMetrica-related components. These services may process information according to their own privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

The game is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, contact us so that we can review the request.

Data Retention

Local gameplay data remains on your device unless you delete the app or reset the data. Advertising and measurement data handled by third-party partners is retained according to those partners' policies.

Your Choices

You can limit ad tracking through iOS privacy settings. You can also delete local gameplay data by removing the app from your device.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer through the support channel provided on the App Store product page.
